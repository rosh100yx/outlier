# Media Kit & Publishing Partners

Are you a blogger, newsletter writer, Substack author, or tech journalist? We want to help you write about Outlier, AI Deskilling, and the Future of Work—and we want to give you full credit for it!

## 🤝 Our Philosophy
We believe the community drives the narrative. If you write an article, analysis, or review of Outlier, you become an official **Publishing Partner**. 

**What you get:**
1. **Direct Credit:** We will link your article directly in our `README.md` and our [Academic Thesis Paper](../paper/measuring-ai-use.md).
2. **Traffic:** We actively direct developers reading our docs to read our partners' analyses.

## 📝 Story Angles & Hooks you can use:
Here are some highly engaging concepts to write about:
- **The "Vibe Coding" Trap:** How AI creates an "Illusion of Competence" and why developers are terrified of losing their ability to code from scratch.
- **The Thermal Receipt:** Why developers are sharing brutalist, terminal-generated "receipts" to prove they still understand their code architecture.
- **The Paradox of Expertise:** Why you need to be a senior engineer to safely use AI, and how junior engineers are failing to build "pattern libraries."
